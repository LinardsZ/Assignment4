Classic Homemade Lasagna

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with our classic lasagna recipe! Layers of perfectly cooked noodles, savory ground beef simmered in rich tomato sauce, and creamy ricotta cheese, all topped with a golden blanket of melted mozzarella and Parmesan. This timeless favorite is a labor of love, but each bite is worth it. Whether you're serving a hungry family or craving leftovers for days, our homemade lasagna promises to satisfy every craving. Get ready to savor the warmth and flavors of a homemade Italian classic – it's guaranteed to become a staple in your recipe repertoire!

prep time

30 min.

cook time

40-45 min.

serving

8 servings

Tags: italian homemade pasta lasagna food

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

step 2

In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

step 3

In a skillet, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, cook until softened. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if necessary. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

step 4

In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese and egg until well mixed.

step 5

Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the baking dish. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a third of the shredded mozzarella cheese and a third of the Parmesan cheese. Repeat this layering process - meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Top with a final layer of meat sauce and remaining shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

step 6

Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, making sure it's tightly sealed around the edges to prevent drying. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.

step 7

Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

step 8

Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!

ingredients

9 lasagna noodles

1 pound (450g) ground beef

1 onion

2 garlic cloves

1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes

1 can (6 oz) tomato paste

1 teaspoon dried basil

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

2 cups (475 ml) ricotta cheese

1 egg

2 cups (200g) mozzarella cheese

1/2 cup (50g) parmesan cheese

fresh parsley

olive oil

tools

Large Pot

Skillet

Mixing bowls

Baking dish

Aluminum foil

Cooking spray
